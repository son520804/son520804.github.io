About

I am In Son Zeng. I am from Macau, a lovely and historical place with area as small as about 20 square miles. During my undergraduate study, I majored in Mathematics, with a specialization in mathematical modeling and statistics modeling at the University of Macau.

My interests in interdisciplinary studies sparked my passion to participate in various international contests for mathematical modeling, including COMAP 2017 (Awarded Agent-based Modelling Paper), CUMCM 2016 and datathon contests.

I pursued master's program of Applied Statistics at the University of Michigan, a university of much relevance to my academic growth! I started to develop genuene passion to Bayesian Statistics, Educational Statistics, and Machine Learning. In the realm of education, I was thrilled in how to design course materials, record time-coded live coding demonstrations and produce inspirational MOOCs in Coursera such as the Applied Data Science in Python series. This is exactly the primary reason why I have always dreamed to work with professor Christopher Brooks and his research team!

I also taught STATS 412 and recorded videos for instructional support. See Regression 1, Regression 2 and Regression 3

A passionate, caring and diligent person with genuine love to statistics. That is who I am.
